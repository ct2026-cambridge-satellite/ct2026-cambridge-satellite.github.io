  <section id="about" class="card">
    <h2>About the Event</h2>
    <p>
      Since the <a href="https://ct2026.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">International Category Theory Conference 2026</a> is a hybrid event
      this year, we invite remote participants to join us at the University of
      Cambridge for the CT2026 Cambridge Satellite Event. In collaboration with the CT2026 organizers, we will stream talks
      from the main conference and support participants who are giving their
      talks online. Alongside the academic programme, we will provide
      coffee breaks, an excursion, and other opportunities to connect in person.
    </p>
  </section>

  <section id="practical-information" class="card">

    <h2>Punting Excursion</h2>
    <p>
      We welcome all participants to join us for a punting excursion on Tuesday
      morning along the river Cam through some of the University's historic
      colleges. We will be meeting at 10:15 at Scudamore's Punting Company. The
      trip should conclude around midday.
    </p>
    <p>
      <strong>Meeting point:</strong>
      <a href="https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Quayside+Punting+Station%2C+Magdalene+St%2C+Cambridge+CB5+8AB%2C+UK" target="_blank" rel="noopener">
        Quayside Punting Station, Magdalene St, Cambridge CB5 8AB, UK
      </a>
      <br>
      <strong>Date:</strong>
      Tuesday 14 July 2026 at 10:15am (BST)
    </p>
